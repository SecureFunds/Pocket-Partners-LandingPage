Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is this expense-sharing app?

Our app is a tool that allows you to easily split and share expenses with your friends, family, or colleagues. Whether you're planning a trip, sharing a bill, or dividing household expenses, the app helps you keep a clear record of who owes what.

How do I add a new expense?

To add a new expense, follow these steps:

1. Log in to the app.

2. Go to the "New Expense by Group" section.

3. Enter the details of the expense: the name, the total amount, and who paid it.

4. Select the group of friends you want to share the expense with. If you don't have a group created, you can create one at this step.

5. The app will automatically divide the expense among all group members according to the settings you choose (equally, percentages, etc.).

6. Save the expense, and all group members will receive a notification.

How are expenses divided?

You can divide expenses in several ways:

- Equal shares: All participants pay the same amount.

- By percentages: Assign a percentage of the total to each person.

- By specific amounts: You can assign an exact amount that each person should pay.

What happens if one of my friends doesn't pay?

The app allows you to send reminders to those with outstanding payments. You can schedule automatic reminders or send them manually from the "Pending Debts" section.

How can I settle a debt?

To settle a debt:

1. Go to the "Debts" section.

2. Select the debt you wish to settle.

3. Choose the payment option (cash, bank transfer, PayPal, etc.) and confirm the payment.

4. Once the payment is received, mark the debt as "Paid."

Is my expense information secure?

Yes, security is our top priority. All data is stored securely. Additionally, your financial information is never shared with third parties.

Can I use the app offline?

The app requires an internet connection to synchronize and share expenses. However, you can enter new expenses offline, and they will automatically synchronize when you reconnect to the internet.

Is there any cost to use the app?

The app offers a free plan with all basic features. We also have a premium plan with additional features such as ad removal, advanced reporting, and priority support.

2. Select "Delete Account."
